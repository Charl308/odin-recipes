Fresh Pasta

Description

This is one of my favourite recipes that I make at least once a week

You can make this with the basic ingredients you almost always have in your home

Ingredients

  • 2 Free-range eggs
  • 100g Semolina wheat
  • 100g Cake wheat flower
  • 2 Tbs Olive Oil
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Add the semoilna, cake wheat flower, and salt into a large mixing bowl
  2. Make a bowl/crater in the middle of the mixture
  3. Add the eggs in the middle of the bowl/crater and mix in slowly using a fork
  4. When you've incorporated most of the eggs into the mixture, add the olive oil and mix it into the mixture with your hands
  5. Turn out mixture onto a slightly flowered surface and knead for 5-7 minutes
